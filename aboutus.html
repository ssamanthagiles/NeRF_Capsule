InternVerse is a platform that allows interns to create and share 3D content
using Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF). It provides tools for generating, editing,
and visualizing 3D scenes, making it accessible for both professionals and
enthusiasts in the field of computer graphics and virtual reality.
